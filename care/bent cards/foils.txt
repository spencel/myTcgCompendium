Foils bend because they are made out of two different materials that react differntly to moisture.
Use desiccant packs in boxes for all cards, especially foils, in order to keep the air dry.
Double sleeve cards, especially foils, to mitigate contact with air.
Bake used desiccant packs every 2 to 6 months at 120 deg C for 2 hours.
Place bent cards in text books between pages and pile on more heavy books or other objects to ameliorate warping.
